About:

My name is Carina and I live in the neighborhood, and happen to have a serious passion for both cats and web development! Lucky for me I also have quite a few cat loving neighbors that are willing to share their precious kitties with me! My hope is that my neighbors can use this site to get to know all the neighborhood kitties. In future iterations I would like to create a full stack web app, where owners can upload their kitties on their own. For now please use the form to submit your kitty with their name, photo, and bio, as well as your name, and I will be sure to add them to our CATalog.
