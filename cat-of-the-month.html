Cat of the Month!


Rhea

Rhea is a very rambunctious and naughty black kitty. She always keeps her human on her toes. Things Rhea has done over the past month include: getting stuck in the neighbor's yard, getting stuck on the neighbor's roof, getting stuck on her own roof, going into the washing machine, going into the dryer, knocking over the air filter, and generally causing havoc.
